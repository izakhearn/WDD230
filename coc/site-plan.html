Clear Water Chamber Of Commerce

Site Plan

Izak Hearn

WDD 230

Overview

Site Name

Clear Water Chamber of Commerce. The site name was chosen based upon the fact that it resides in Clear Water County and serves that community.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show what the Clear Water Chamber of Commerce can do for its members. It will showcase all the latest events and news that is happening in the county. It will showcase who in the community are members and have a directory that can be used to look up members of the chamber and their contact details. There will also be the ability so discover events and what the chamber does for the community. There will be a join page where it shows the advantage of being a member of the chamber and have a form to fill in and submit to be contacted in order to join.

Scenarios

  • What events does the chamber have coming up?
  • What does the chamber do for the community
  • Who are members of the chamber ?
  • How can I join the chamber ?
  • Who are the directors of the chamber?
  • How can I contact the chamber?
